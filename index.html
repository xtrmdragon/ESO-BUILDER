Beginner's Guide to Crafting

Step 1
Start trait research immediately on your main crafter because research time becomes the biggest long-term gate.
Step 2
For Blacksmithing, Clothing, and Woodworking, deconstruction is usually your main leveling engine, while Alchemy and Provisioning grow quickly through crafting consumables.
Step 3
Do daily crafting writs once unlocked for steady inspiration, materials, surveys, and long-term value.
Step 4
Pick one primary crafter first so your skill points and research timers stay focused instead of spreading everything too thin.
Step 5
Harvest everything while questing. Even if you do not need the materials yet, they build your future crafting stockpile.
